We are an AI and Automation agency working for creative artists.

We created Leonardo in 2023 because we could see the huge opportunities that AI presents to support a successful creative sector.
But we could also see the huge risk that AI would bypass the sector, as technology companies always seem to focus on large commercial enterprises.
We decided to fill the gap, to create AI applications designed specifically for artists, makers, organisers and other creatives.
Our mission is to help the creative sector to thrive, by providing AI solutions which are:
Liberating – takes care of the admin so the creator has more time for their art or craft
Elevating – raises the profile of the artist or maker, especially on social media
Accessible – affordable and easy to use
